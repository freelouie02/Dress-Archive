Dark Winter
Your color season — rich, cool, and high-contrast
What is Dark Winter? Dark Winter sits at the cool-dark crossroads of Winter and Autumn. Your best colors are deeply saturated, cool-to-neutral undertoned, and high-contrast. Think midnight navy, true black, icy white, burgundy, forest green, plum, and deep jewel tones. Warm muted earth tones and pastels tend to wash you out.
